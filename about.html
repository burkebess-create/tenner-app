What is Tenner?

Tenner is a social app for making and sharing top 10 lists — your favorite movies, restaurants, songs, books, vacation spots, anything. See how your picks compare with friends and groups, react to each other's lists, and discover new things through the people you trust.

How it works

Make a list

Pick a category — movies, restaurants, songs — and rank your top 10.

Share with friends

Send your list to friends or post it to a group to see how everyone ranks the same things.

Compare side by side

See which picks overlap, which surprise you, and what's at the top across your circle.

Blind fill

Group members fill out their lists without seeing each other's — then reveal together.

Why we built it

Group chats are full of "what should we watch?" and "best place for tacos?" — but everyone has different tastes and there's no easy way to compare. Tenner turns those debates into structured, shareable lists that surface what your people actually love.

What data we collect

To make Tenner work, we collect your email and (if you sign in with Google) your name and profile picture. We store the lists, friends, and groups you create. We may use this data to suggest gift ideas based on friends' lists, show app-wide aggregated insights, and personalize recommendations. We never sell your raw personal data.
