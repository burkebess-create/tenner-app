What is Tenner?

Tenner is a social app for making and sharing top 10 lists — your favorite movies, restaurants, songs, books, vacation spots, anything. See how your picks compare with friends and groups, react to each other's lists, and discover new things through the people you trust.

How it works

Make a list

Pick a category — movies, restaurants, songs, or create your own custom category — and rank your top 10.

Share with friends

Send your list to friends or post it to a group to see how everyone ranks the same things.

Compare side by side

See which picks overlap, which surprise you, and what's at the top across your circle.

Blind fill

Choose whether group members fill out their lists without seeing each other's — then reveal together — or see each other's picks from the start.

What Tenner is all about

The best relationships are built on shared interests, learning about (and from) each other, and a healthy dose of friendly trash talk about whose favorites are clearly superior. Tenner brings friends, family, and groups together to discover what you have in common, learn what makes each other tick, and spark the kind of conversations that make every relationship and group dynamic better. And along the way, by sharing and comparing lists with the people you know, you might just stumble onto your new favorite movie, TV show, restaurant, book, or you-fill-in-the-blank.

What data we collect

To make Tenner work, we collect your email and (if you sign in with Google) your name and profile picture. We store the lists, friends, and groups you create. We may use this data to suggest gift ideas based on friends' lists, show app-wide aggregated insights, and personalize recommendations. We never sell your raw personal data. For full details, see Tenner's Privacy Policy and Tenner's Terms of Service. By using Tenner, you agree to both.
